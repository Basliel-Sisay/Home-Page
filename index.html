About Me

As a third-year Software Engineering student at Addis Ababa University, I've cultivated a deep passion for building inclusive, efficient, and user-centered digital experiences. My journey began with a fascination for how code shapes the way people interact with technology, and over time, I've developed strong proficiency in languages like Python, C++, JavaScript, and TypeScript. What drives me is the opportunity to solve real world problems through clean, maintainable code and thoughtful design especially when it comes to accessibility and inclusive development. I find joy in mentoring junior developers, breaking down complex concepts into practical, analogy driven guides, and creating web interfaces that are not only functional but welcoming to all users. Whether I'm refining semantic markup, optimizing keyboard navigation, or crafting meaningful alt text, I'm always striving to make the web a more usable and equitable space.
